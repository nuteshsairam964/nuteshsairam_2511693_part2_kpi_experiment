\# Recommendation Memo



\## Executive Summary



This analysis evaluated the performance of a new onboarding campaign using an A/B experiment. The objective was to determine whether the Treatment group improved the Paid Conversion Rate compared to the Control group while ensuring that important guardrail metrics remained stable.



\## North Star Metric



The selected North Star Metric is \*\*Paid Conversion Rate\*\*, as it directly measures the percentage of users who become paying customers and has the greatest impact on business growth.



\## Experiment Results



The Treatment group achieved a Paid Conversion Rate of \*\*7.04%\*\*, while the Control group achieved \*\*3.19%\*\*.



A one-tailed hypothesis test was performed.



\* Z Statistic: \*\*3.264\*\*

\* P-value: \*\*0.00055\*\*

\* Significance Level: \*\*0.05\*\*



Since the p-value is less than 0.05, the null hypothesis was rejected.



\## Guardrail Metrics



The following guardrail metrics were evaluated:



\* Refund Rate

\* Support Ticket Rate

\* Average Days to Convert

\* Average Engagement Score



These metrics were reviewed to ensure that improvements in conversion did not negatively affect customer experience.



\## Recommendation



Based on the analysis, the Treatment group significantly improved the Paid Conversion Rate compared to the Control group.



Since the statistical test showed a significant improvement and the guardrail metrics were evaluated as part of the decision-making process, the recommendation is to \*\*launch the Treatment experience\*\*.



\## Risks and Limitations



\* Duplicate user records were identified and removed before analysis.

\* Some missing values were present in segmentation-related columns.

\* Revenue contained a few outliers, which should continue to be monitored.

\* Future experiments can include additional customer segments and longer observation periods.



\## Next Steps



1\. Launch the Treatment experience.

2\. Continue monitoring guardrail metrics after rollout.

3\. Track long-term customer retention and revenue.

4\. Perform additional experiments to further optimize onboarding.
